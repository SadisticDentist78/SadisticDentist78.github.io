Five HTML Tags and their descriptions.

Some of the tags below were used in here

The List:

Blockquote
- Creates an eye-catching block of text, suitable for quotes.
Citation
- Often paired with quotations, used to highlight the source of the information like person or book.
Technical Term
- Makes symbols be written in cursive.
Superscript Text
- Makes symbols supesript, ideal for dates.
Horizontal Line
- Creates the line to separate topics.
